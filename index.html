Temple Guidelines

Non-compliance will result in entry denial at checkpoint gates.

--

--

Dress Code

Traditional Indian attire is mandatory. Gents must wear dhotis or pyjamas; sarees or chudidars with dupatta for ladies.

Prohibited Items

Mobile phones, cameras, electronic wearables, and memory devices are banned beyond outer checkpoints. Free lockers are available outside.

Required Documents

Original physical Aadhaar card matching your booking token must be carried. Digital copies are not accepted inside security bays.

Code of Conduct

Maintain silence inside sanctum. Follow queue discipline. Avoid carrying food items. Respect the sacred space and fellow pilgrims.

Prasadam & Food

Free prasadam is distributed at designated counters. Outside food and beverages are not permitted inside the temple premises.

Accessibility

Wheelchair assistance available at main entry. Separate queues for elderly, differently-abled, and families with infants.
